# Privacy Policy for TikRocket

Last updated: May 15, 2026

SentixApps ("us", "we", or "our") operates the TikRocket mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

### Types of Data Collected

#### Personal Data
While using our Service, we do not require you to provide us with personally identifiable information like your name or phone number. However, we use **Firebase Anonymous Authentication**, which creates a unique, temporary identifier to manage your user session and store your progress securely across your usage session.

#### Usage Data
We may also collect information that your mobile device sends whenever you use our Service. This may include information such as your device's IP address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app, the time and date of your use, and other statistics.

## 4. Security

## 5. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.
